The Vancouver Grammar Summer Programme (VGSP) is providing flexible and customized study program; and feature a wide variety of offerings for students of different age. We have two weeks, three weeks, and four weeks summer Programs which will be run by our talented and dedicated VGS teachers, while some specialized camps feature outside instructors such as outdoor expansion, outdoor survival summer camp, music tour, ice hockey tour, art sketch tour, robot camp.
