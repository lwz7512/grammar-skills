Personal English Tutors

The one-on-one interaction in a tutoring session promotes a positive learning environment.

Vancouver Grammar School offers excellent English language tutoring for individuals whether you’re a beginner or advanced or just need help with accent reduction—we’ve got you covered!

Our one-to-one sessions are completely tailored to your needs; choose a topic or skill that you want to improve or select from a learning plan. If you’re not sure, you can get expert recommendations from your tutor. After every online lesson, your tutor will leave you detailed feedback on your speaking performance.

Our online English tutors have years of experience teaching English and understand the skills needed to succeed. Each tutor holds a teaching qualification and has also worked in a professional setting in diverse sectors.
